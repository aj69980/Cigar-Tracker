Arturo Fuente Hemingway is one of the most celebrated and sought-after cigars in the free world. Created by master cigar-maker, Carlito Fuente, these cigars are rolled in grand fashion using only the finest Dominican tobaccos and choicest Cameroon wrappers. Medium-bodied in strength, these lovelies are richly flavored boasting luscious notes of earth, spice and brown sugar. Available in a range of unique sizes, these super-deluxe perfectos are beautifully elegant and delicious.
